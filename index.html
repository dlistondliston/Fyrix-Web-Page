Why Fyrix exists

Medical-device discipline, delivered in an SME-friendly way

Fyrix was conceived to pass on the skills and disciplines developed in the medical device industry — an environment where continuous improvement is not optional, but essential. Through over 40 years of combined experience in a billion-dollar medical device manufacturer, we’ve benefited from extensive training in process excellence, problem solving, and data-driven improvement, driven by a highly regulated and performance-critical sector.

Large organisations can afford permanent teams to drive continuous improvement. We understand that this level of resource is harder to sustain in small and medium-sized businesses. Fyrix bridges that gap by delivering enterprise-level capability in practical, affordable steps.
